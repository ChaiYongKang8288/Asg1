Micheal Jordan

Michael Jeffery Jordan was born on February 17th 1963 and was raised by his parents, James and Delores Jordan and he grew up in Wilmington,North Carolina where growing up he was never really a basketball player instead he was a baseball player which his father wanted but Michael always preffered to play basketball and his basketball career took off when he was part of the 1981-1982 University of North Carolina where they won the NCAA tournament where he took the final shot and sealed it which made him one of the most popular draft prospects in the 1984 NBA draft. Before he was in the NBA, he had signed a deal with Nike which made Air Jordans which are a very popular sneaker till now. After the Chicago Bulls took him with the 3rd pick in the draft, the rest was history.

Michael Jordan had a 15 year NBA career where many consider him the G.O.A.T of basketball because he has become a household name and during his career, he retired thrice, once in 1993 after his father,James Jordan's death where he went to play professional baseball to fufil his father's dream of him playing baseball, another time in 1998 where the Chicago Bulls decided to give up their roster to rebuild it and Michael Jordan did not want it and the last time in 2002 where he came out of retirement to show he could still play however realised that it was time to give up.

Michael Jordan family consists of his mother,Delores Jordan , his wifes Juanita Vanoy and Yvette Prietto and his children Marcus ,Victoria , Ysabel,Jeffery and Jasmine Jordan.He is also the proud owner of the Charlotte Hornets and has a net worth of 1.7 billion dollars as of 2022.

In his 15 year career , Michael Jordan accumulated 32292 points,6672 rebounds,5633 assists,2514 steals and 893 blocks.He averaged 30.1 points,6.2 rebounds,5.3 assists and 2.3 steals per game for his career. His accolades consists of Rookie of the Year; Five-time NBA MVP; Six-time NBA champion; Six-time NBA Finals MVP; Ten-time All-NBA First Team; Nine time NBA All-Defensive First Team; Defensive Player of the Year; 14-time NBA All-Star; Three-time NBA All-Star MVP; 50th Anniversary All-Time Team; 75th Anniversary All-time Team; Ten scoring titles . Which allowed Michael Jordan to be inducted into the Basketball Hall Of Fame in 2009.
